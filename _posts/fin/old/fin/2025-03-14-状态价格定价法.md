要先计算 :

$$P_A = \pi_uUP_A + \pi_dDP_A$$

$$\pi_u=\frac{1-de^{-r_f}}{u-d}$$

$$\pi_d=\frac{ue^{r_f}-1}{u-d}$$

可计算 $P_B$

$$P_B = \pi_uUP_B + \pi_dDP_B$$
